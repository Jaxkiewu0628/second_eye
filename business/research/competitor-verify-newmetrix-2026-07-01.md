# Competitor verification — Newmetrix (FKA Smartvid.io) — 2026-07-01

**In a line:** Adversarially verified the "Newmetrix" worker-safety player. Verdict: **CONFIRMED but stale** — it's a cloud, software-only, *after-the-fact / predictive* jobsite-image safety-analytics tool (Camp A), and it no longer exists as an independent company (Oracle absorbed its assets + IP in **Oct 2022**, folded into Oracle Construction Intelligence Cloud). Leaves our "cheap + wearable + offline personal real-time warning" lane wide open.

## Claim under test
Input claim: "Newmetrix = pure software + ingests fixed/inspection jobsite photos/video; offline=no; custom pricing by data volume; Camp A (pure AI vision); analyzes site imagery for 100+ hazard/PPE violations + predicts high-risk projects; fully cloud, after-the-fact/near-real-time; safety-management/compliance tool, different lane from us."

## Verdict: CONFIRMED (with two corrections that make it *weaker* as a competitor)
Every attribute in the claim checks out against primary + strong secondary sources. Two additions:
1. **It's not an independent player anymore.** Oracle acquired the assets/IP of Smartvid.io (d/b/a Newmetrix) on **01-Oct-2022**; founders + key leaders joined Oracle; product is now embedded in **Oracle Construction Intelligence Cloud** as an Oracle SKU. The "standalone competitor" is effectively gone — it's an enterprise Oracle module.
2. **Cameras aren't even required.** It is *hardware-agnostic* — it ingests photos/videos crews already capture for project documentation (Procore, Autodesk BIM 360, Oracle Aconex, StructionSite, OxBlue site cameras). So it's even further from "wearable device on the worker" than the claim implies.

## Camp
Camp A (pure AI vision analytics on jobsite imagery). Squarely a **safety-management / compliance / risk-prediction** tool for the office — NOT a wearer-facing device. Different lane from Second Eye.

## leaves_gap_for_us — YES (wide open)
Newmetrix (and now Oracle CIC) attacks safety from the **management/compliance** side: cloud, after-the-fact, project-level risk prediction from images crews already shot. It does **nothing** on the axis Second Eye owns:
- **cheap** (~$141 BOM) vs enterprise Oracle contract;
- **worn by the individual worker** vs no device at all;
- **offline / on-device** vs cloud;
- **real-time personal warning** ("obstacle/hazard ahead" to *this* worker, now) vs passive observation for a manager later.
It's complementary, not competitive. Our lane is untouched.
